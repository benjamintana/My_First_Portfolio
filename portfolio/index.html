Introduction

Hey there! I am Benjamin Barimah-Sarpong, a Ghanaian front-end web developer and data analyst. With a passion for creating visually appealing, user-friendly websites and turning raw data into meaningful insights, I bring a unique blend of creativity and analytical thinking to every project I take on. I specialize in building responsive web interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks, while also leveraging tools like Excel, SQL, and Python for data analysis and visualization. Whether it's designing intuitive user experiences or interpreting complex data sets, I thrive on solving real-world problems through technology. Welcome to my portfolio — feel free to explore my work, and don’t hesitate to get in touch!
